    Ethics & AI interpretation statement

    Animated guides are interpretation, not resurrection

    This project does not claim to recreate ancient Egyptians as they truly looked, sounded, or behaved. Animated figures are educational guide interfaces for discussing objects, sites, and reconstruction uncertainty.

    Source-based, reconstructed, and speculative parts must remain visibly labeled. AI-generated motion or narration should be reviewed before public exhibition.
